What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds. You invest a fixed amount monthly and benefit from the power of compounding over time.

How SIP Returns Are Calculated?

SIP returns are calculated using a compounding formula that considers monthly investment, expected annual return rate and total investment duration in months.

SIP Formula

Future Value = P × [((1+r)^n − 1) / r] × (1+r)
